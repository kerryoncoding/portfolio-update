Medical Devices

It has been very rewarding to be part of the development of life saving medical devices. I have gathered insights through usability research studies as well as product validation labs.

I had the pleasure of working directly with many doctors, nurses, surgeons, and heard direct feedback from many patients about medical devices.

This transport incubator meets global standards for use in helicopters and ambulances.

In addition to designing many aspects of this unit, I was part of product verification testing, including IPx, shock, and vibration testing.
